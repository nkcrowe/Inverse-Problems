# Brownian Motion Notes

## Definition

$$
\{W_t\}_{t \ge 0}
$$

A Brownian motion satisfies:

- $W_0 = 0$
- Independent increments
- $\Delta W \sim N(0, \Delta t)$
- Continuous paths

## Increments

$$
\Delta W = W_{t+\Delta t} - W_t
$$

$$
\Delta W \sim N(0, \Delta t)
$$

- Size of increment: $\sim \sqrt{\Delta t}$
